what i offer

Indulge in the delightful world of conchas like never before with my artisanal conchas. I specialize in crafting these beloved Mexican sweet bread treasures, making every moment a special occasion. Whether you're celebrating a birthday, anniversary, or simply craving a delicious treat, I have you covered.

What sets me apart is my commitment to inclusivity and customization. I understand that dietary preferences and restrictions vary, which is why I offer a wide range of options to cater to everyone's needs. My menu features traditional conchas, along with gluten-free, vegan, sugar-free, and lactose-free alternatives, ensuring that everyone can savor the joy of conchas without worry.

Looking for something unique and personalized? I'm here to make your concha dreams come true. My bakery welcomes custom orders, allowing you to choose your favorite flavors, colors, and designs for conchas that perfectly match your special event or celebration.

about me

I'm a dedicated ER nurse who found an unexpected passion in the world of custom artisanal conchas. Balancing the intensity of my nursing career with the sweetness of concha creation has been an exciting journey. It all began as a hobby, but my love for crafting these delicious treats led to the birth of my own concha bakery. I take pride in bringing joy through personalized concha creations. Join me on this delightful adventure where I'm making moments sweeter one concha at a time.

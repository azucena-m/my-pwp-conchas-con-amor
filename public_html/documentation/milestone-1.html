Milestone 1 for Personal Website Project

Purpose, Audience, Goal

Purpose

The purpose of this project is to utilize different programming languages to create a fluid working website that will showcase artisanal Mexican conchas.

Audience

The website will be created with the common consumer in mind. The proposed audience consists of customers of all ages with an interest on Mexican comfort foods.

Goal

The goal of my PWP is to increase sales and online traffic for a starting small business.

Persona

Name: Mike Arisma

Description:

Tech:

  • Mike uses the latest Iphone 15 iOS 16.6.1 with Chrome as browser.
  • Uses a MacBook Air with an M2 chip, with 8GB RAM, and Chrome as browser.
  • Uses Spotify to stream music.

Behaviors and Attitudes

  • Mike is a foodie wanting to experience the world through different culinary experiences.
  • He loves desserts and parties, particularly, the ones that are culturally significant.
  • He uses technology as a means to find new restaurants/food businesses.
  • Mike dislikes websites that are not fluid.
  • He enjoys browsing websites that provide an eye-catching display.
  • Mike hates websites and apps that are buggy or that do not adjust well to phone browsers.

Proficiency

  • Mike is a proficient technology user.
  • He utilizes technology as a way to document favorite businesses and not-so-favorite businesses.

Frustrations

  • Mike gets easily frustrated when a food website does not load properly.
  • He loses interest in websites that are not visually appealing, that are "boring", or that appear chaotic.
  • He dislikes business websites that do not "flow".

Goal

Mike's ultimate goal is to find a business that carries artisanal personalized conchas for his themed party. He is interested in buying a large quantity of mini conchas to serve to his guests.

User Story

As a consumer, I want to access a website that is fluid and attractive where I can clearly find what I am looking for--conchas..
